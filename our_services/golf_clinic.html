Our tailored corporate golf seminar and clinic program is specially designed to meet the company's event objectives. 

All participants will be attended by our golf professionals all the time in all area of golf learning, enhancement and improving lessons. They will also be entertained by our selected professionals to perform trick shots. 

There will be classroom lecturing on the golf subjects depending on the playing level of the participants on: 

  • How to select the right iron club? What golfers need to know?
  • How to select and buy a driver to get your optimum tee-shot? Why is it important?
  • Understanding the ball flight law can help golfers to draw or fade the shot performance.
  • How to lower a current player's score? What golfer should intensify?
  • Etiquette and Rules of golf.
  • Mental skills for golf
  • Tips for improving golf skills
